book-summary Christian reflections during a crisis

This title was written during the corona-virus global pandemic, a time when the whole world was forced into a lockdown, the author took the opportunity to reflect and write down some of the reflections He had, which can be organized into four groups of thoughts, namely : Faith , Prayer , Relationships and unconventional spiritual ideas. This book is one of a kind because its Devotional and an Autobiography, all at once.
